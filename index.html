Mission

A modern, industry-relevant slice of AI, taught with real constraints.

We focus on practical systems: data quality, evaluation, iteration loops, and shipping workflows. Members learn by building and reviewing each other’s work.

What We Do

  • Workshops on ML fundamentals, LLMs, and data workflows
  • Research reading groups designed for beginners
  • Project teams, mentorship, demos, and portfolio-ready deliverables
